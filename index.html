What happens to a relationship under load?

Dear reader,

It usually starts with something small.

A minor disagreement turns into some silent treatment. Maybe your text goes unanswered for hours. Your partner seems more distant than usual. Conversations become shorter. You begin to think: Did I do something wrong?

No relationship is perfect. The truth is that every close relationship experiences moments of stress. Whether it's a friendship, family relationship, or a romantic partnership, everyone faces challenges that can create wear and tear over time. In fact, these situations often shape how we view ourselves and the people closest to us.

That's exactly what this website is about. Together, we'll explore the different factors that can place stress on close relationships. Through the various concepts presented, our goal is to make the stresses and strains that affect close relationships more engaging and easier to understand.

We hope this website gives you a better understanding of the challenges that relationships face and encourages you to reflect on the connections you have with the people around you.

Thank you for taking the time to visit!

Sincerely,
