What happens to a relationship under load?

Every relationship gets pulled, pushed, and tested somehow. We borrowed a idea from materials science, the stress and strain curve, to map out what actually happens to a couple or a friendship when the pressure is on: where it bends, where it holds, and where it can snap. This site walks through the psychology behind all of it, one stage at a time.
